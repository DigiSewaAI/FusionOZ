About Fusion OZ

Your Trusted Partner for Australian Migration & Education

Fusion OZ was established in 2009 with the vision to develop education and immigration services that help individuals who dream to migrate to Australia to enhance themselves to become their best version.

We are one of the leading Education and Migration Agencies in Sydney, Australia with branch offices in Melbourne CBD, Malaysia, China, and Nepal. We are committed to providing customized and professional support to our clients throughout their whole education or migration journey in Australia.

Our team consists of MARA Agent Yan Zhang with over 18 years of experience, and a dedicated group of consultants with a wide range of expertise. We strive to deliver premier services to our clients to change their definition of the Australian experience.

At Fusion OZ Nepal, we understand the challenges of moving to a new country. Our personalized approach ensures that each client receives tailored solutions for their unique situation.

Our Services

Education Consulting

We help international students find the right course for their future career goals. We manage all aspects of the application procedure and are with you every step of the way from course selection to visa approval.

Migration Services

Expert guidance on all visa categories including skilled migration, family visas, and permanent residency. Our MARA-registered agents ensure professional advice and high success rates for your visa applications.

Investment Consulting

Strategic advice for business and investment opportunities in Australia. We guide you through the complex process of investment migration and help you establish successful ventures in Australia.
